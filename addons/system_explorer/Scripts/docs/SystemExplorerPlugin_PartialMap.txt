Temporary states such as a missing current editor/TextEdit, a not-yet-current unique final Script, or a not-yet-listed final Script are retried without an unbounded queue; definitive ambiguity, missing-file, old-instance, old-path, duplicate-final-path, casing, or buffer-text failures stop immediately.

A successful filesystem rename is never rolled back because of a later editor-state failure.
